Why Fertilizer Management Matters

Fertilizers supply essential nutrients required for healthy plant growth. Incorrect fertilizer usage can lead to nutrient imbalance, soil degradation, reduced yield, and increased production costs. Smart fertilizer management ensures optimal crop nutrition and soil health.
